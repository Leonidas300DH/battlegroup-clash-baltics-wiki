## Playthroughs — Scenario 1 (Joe Boyle - Hexbyhex)

A multi-part playthrough of Scenario 1 ("A Bump In The Road") by [Joe Boyle - Hexbyhex](https://www.youtube.com/@Hexbyhex). Covers the complete game from Turn 2 through Turn 4 with detailed commentary on rules, tactics, and decision-making.

| # | Video | Link |
|---|-------|------|
| 1 | **Turn 2: Introduction and Electronic Warfare** | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=idBybyp1ToE) |
| 2 | **Turn 2 (Pt 2): Initial Russian advances** | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2As-hZ1EJv8) |
| 3 | **Turn 2 (Pt 3): Both sides taking losses as Russians push on** | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ErvY5JEMOYc) |
| 4 | **Turn 3: The Russians start to suffer heavy losses** | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Mbyu3GJnx6U) |
| 5 | **Turn 4: A desperate Russian assault... does it succeed?** | [Watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NJyuAvzSE2c) |

> [!tip]
> This series is the best way to see the game in action. Joe walks through the [[electronic-warfare|Electronic Warfare (EW)]] phase, [[initiative|Initiative]] rolls, activation decisions, [[direct-fire|Direct Fire]] resolution, and [[assault|Assault]] mechanics step by step.
