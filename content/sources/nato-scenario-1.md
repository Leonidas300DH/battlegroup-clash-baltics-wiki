# NATO Scenario 1 — A Bump In The Road

**File:** `raw/NATO SCENARIO 1.pdf`
**Pages:** 3
**Side:** [[nato|NATO]]
**Scenario:** [[scenario-1-bump-in-the-road|Scenario 1 — A Bump In The Road]]

## Briefing Summary

The Russian invasion has begun. Russian forces are pushing southwest to cut off NATO's northern forces — a move NATO did not anticipate. NATO must fight a **delaying action** to prevent the enemy from reaching the bridge at Sindi (E220/N234), giving engineers time to blow it.

- No artillery support available (deployed elsewhere)
- Mortar platoon and organic drones provide indirect fire
- Forces are stretched; avoid attrition but not at the expense of holding the bridge

## NATO [[glossary|Order of Battle (ORBAT)]] (Scenario 1)

See [[scenario-1-bump-in-the-road#NATO ORBAT]] for the formatted [[glossary|ORBAT]].

Key features:
- All NATO [[force-elements|Force Elements (FEs)]] begin at **Resilience 3** (not the usual 4) — representing under-manned platoons
- All non-Mounted Foot [[force-elements|FEs]] begin with **Shell Scrape** and **Overwatch** markers
- Only 1 Tracked [[force-elements|Platoon (PL)]] (3-A-1 T) + Tac [[force-elements|Headquarters (HQ)]] Embedded in it
- No artillery — only Mortar and [[support-assets|First Person View drone (FPV)]] for indirect fire
- [[atgm|Anti-Tank Guided Missiles (ATGMs)]]: 1x Javelin (1-B-1), rest are NLAWs (1,000m range)

## Commander's Intent

| Objective | Description | Victory Condition |
|-----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Primary** | Deny the enemy the bridge | No Russian [[force-elements|PL]]-sized [[force-elements|FE]] at E220/N234 at end of Turn 5 |
| **Secondary (1-3)** | Evacuate your forces | No more than 2 NATO [[force-elements|PLs]] Eliminated or south/east of bridge at end of Turn 5 |
| **Secondary (4-6)** | Hold Sindi | At least 1 NATO Foot [[force-elements|PL]] in GS E21/N22 at end of Turn 5 |

## Set-Up Rules

- All NATO [[force-elements|FEs]] begin on the map, placed **east of GL E26**
- Placement happens **after** both sides draft their Sync Matrix
- Any non-Mounted Foot [[force-elements|FE]] may begin with a Shell Scrape marker
- Any NATO [[force-elements|FE]] may begin with an Overwatch marker
- Starting positions (model Sync Matrix): scattered defensive positions covering approaches to the bridge
