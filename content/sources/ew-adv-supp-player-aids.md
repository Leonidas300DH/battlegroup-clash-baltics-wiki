# Player Aids 7 & 8

**File:** `raw/EWAdvSupp_PlayerAids7&8.pdf`
**Pages:** 2

Reference cards for the [[ew-adv-supp-rules|Advanced Rules Supplement]].

## Player Aid 7

Contains:
- **[[advanced-orders|Order Verbs table]]** — all 10 Order verbs with their mechanical effects
- **[[boundary-lines|Boundary Lines]]** summary — the 3 situations that generate [[electronic-warfare|Electronic Transmission (ETX)]]
- **[[advanced-ew|EW Activity]]** sequence — the 5 steps replacing base game EW

## Player Aid 8

Contains:
- **EW Attacks** table — full procedure and effects for all 4 EW Types:
  - SIGINT: places Target markers on identified [[force-elements|Headquarters (HQ)]]/[[force-elements|ISTAR]] [[force-elements|Force Elements (FEs)]]
  - GNSS Jamming: restricts movement to visible locations
  - Drone Jamming: removes [[support-assets|Unmanned Aerial Support drone (UAS)]] [[line-of-sight|Line of Sight (LoS)]] (can self-jam)
  - Comms Jamming: 50% chance ETX-generating actions fail (can self-jam)
- **Target marker** placement and usage rules
- **Target markers & ISTAR** — 3+ markers allow Indirect Fire without ISTAR LoS
