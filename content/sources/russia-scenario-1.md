# Russia Scenario 1 — A Bump In The Road

**File:** `raw/RUSSIA SCENARIO 1.pdf`
**Pages:** 3
**Side:** [[russia|Russia]]
**Scenario:** [[scenario-1-bump-in-the-road|Scenario 1 — A Bump In The Road]]

## Briefing Summary

The invasion is underway. Russian forces have made significant headway, pushing southwest to cut off NATO's northern force. Capturing the bridge north of Sindi (E220/N234) intact is critical — it provides an additional line of communication and supply for westward movement.

- Intelligence indicates NATO has about **half** Russia's force strength guarding the bridge
- NATO has not yet rigged the bridge for demolition, but time is limited
- Speed is essential — overwhelm the defenders and seize the bridge

## Russia [[glossary|Order of Battle (ORBAT)]] (Scenario 1)

See [[scenario-1-bump-in-the-road#Russia ORBAT]] for the formatted [[glossary|ORBAT]].

Key features:
- 3 mechanised infantry [[task-groups|Task Groups (TGps)]] ([[task-groups|TGp]] 1-3), each with 2 Tracked [[force-elements|Platoons (PLs)]] + 2 Mounted Foot [[force-elements|PLs]]
- 1 Reconnaissance (Recce) [[task-groups|Task Group (TGp)]] ([[task-groups|TGp]] 4) with single Recce [[force-elements|Section (SECT)]]
- All Tracked [[force-elements|PLs]] have **Stabber (depleted)** [[atgm|Anti-Tank Guided Missiles (ATGMs)]], all Foot [[force-elements|PLs]] have **RPGs**
- 152mm Artillery with **4+1 = 5 Fire Missions**
- [[support-assets|First Person View drone (FPV)]] with **4+2 = 6 Fire Missions**
- All [[force-elements|Force Elements (FEs)]] begin Mounted, entering from the eastern map edge

## Commander's Intent

| Objective | Description | Victory Condition |
|-----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Primary** | Seize the bridge intact | At least 1 Russian [[force-elements|PL]]-sized [[force-elements|FE]] at E220/N234 at end of Turn 5 |
| **Secondary (1-3)** | Secure Sindi | At least 1 Russian Foot [[force-elements|PL]] in GS E21/N22 at end of Turn 5 |
| **Secondary (4-6)** | Attrit enemy forces | At least 3 NATO [[force-elements|PLs]] (1 must be Tracked) Eliminated or reduced to Resilience 1 |

## Set-Up Rules

- Russian forces enter from **anywhere on the eastern edge** (right) of Map C on Turn 1
- Entry points specified in model Sync Matrix
- All [[force-elements|FEs]] begin Mounted/Embedded as noted in the [[glossary|ORBAT]]
- NATO [[force-elements|FEs]] are placed first (after both sides draft Sync Matrices)

## Scenario-Specific Rules

- Only **one** friendly Tracked [[force-elements|PL]] may cross any Bridge per turn (no limit on [[force-elements|SECTs]])
- Model Sync Matrix provided assumes Secondary Objective = "Secure Sindi"
