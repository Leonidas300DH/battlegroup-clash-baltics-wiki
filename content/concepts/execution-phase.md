# Execution Phase (Phase 3)

The Execution Phase is the **heart of the game** — where forces activate and fight.

## Steps

### 3.1 Determine Initiative

See [[initiative]]. Both sides roll 1D6 + modifiers. The winner decides which side activates first in the Action Round.

### 3.2 Action Round

Sides **alternate** activating a [[task-groups|Task Group]] or [[support-assets|Support Asset (SA)]]:

1. The [[initiative|Initiative]] side decides who goes first
2. The activating side selects a Task Group or SA
3. All [[force-elements|Force Elements (FEs)]] in the Task Group activate one after another, each taking an [[actions-overview|Action]]
4. Play alternates to the other side
5. Continue until both sides have activated all Task Groups and SAs
6. If one side finishes first, the other activates remaining units sequentially

**Passing**: a side may pass instead of activating, but then makes **no more activations** that turn (can still [[overwatch#Reactive Fire|Reactive Fire]]).

**[[command-activation|Command Activation]]** (Standard/Advanced): an HQ can activate additional Task Groups simultaneously.

### 3.3 Clean-up

Apply in order:
1. **Remove** all Smoke, Moved, Activated, and Reorg markers
2. **Rally test** for each FE with Assault Reorg marker — pass removes it, fail flips it to regular Reorg
3. **Flip** Task Group markers to Unactivated side
4. **Return** unused/discarded EW Chits and Target markers to the supply
5. **Advance** Turn Tracker Die by 1

> [!note]
> **Overwatch markers persist** between turns — they are NOT removed in Clean-up. They stay until the FE Reactive Fires, takes a different Action, or receives Reorg.
